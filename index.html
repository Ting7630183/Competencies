Competencies Predictor
This simulator is intended for theoretical use to showcase the power of interventions across the Skill, Character, and Meta-Learning dimensions, and to allow for comparisons which could support decision-makers in best utilizing time and resources. The data is generated through the creation of research-informed “synthetic students,” based upon personality types, typical learning progressions, and other research syntheses. The project’s goal is to showcase the power in the application of data science to education fields, and the potential learning gains if these premises became solidified through studies of real-world students and classrooms.
